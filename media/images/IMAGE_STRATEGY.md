# Image Strategy for Paxi Technologies Website

## Recommended Image Placements

### 1. Hero Section (Homepage)
**Location:** `/index.html` - Hero section
**Recommended:** 
- Professional IT/business image (1920x1080px)
- Examples: Modern office, team collaboration, technology workspace
- Format: JPG/WebP, optimized
- **File:** `hero-main.jpg` or `hero-background.jpg`

### 2. Who We Serve Section
**Location:** `/index.html` - "Built for Small and Mid-Sized Businesses"
**Recommended:**
- Small business office or team image
- Healthcare setting (if focusing on healthcare)
- Diverse business environments
- **File:** `business-environment.jpg` (1200x800px)

### 3. Service Cards Enhancement
**Location:** `/index.html` - Service routing section
**Current:** SVG icons only
**Enhancement:** Add background images or larger illustrations
- IT Support: Network/server room image
- Project Management: Team planning/coordination image  
- Consulting: Strategy meeting/image
- **Files:** `service-support-bg.jpg`, `service-project-bg.jpg`, `service-consulting-bg.jpg`

### 4. About Page
**Location:** `/about/index.html`
**Recommended:**
- Team photo (if available)
- Office/workspace image
- Professional headshots (optional)
- **Files:** `team-photo.jpg`, `office-space.jpg`

### 5. Service Pages
**Location:** `/services/*/index.html`
**Recommended:**
- IT Support: Help desk/technical support image
- Project Management: Project planning/coordination image
- Consulting: Strategy/consultation image
- **Files:** `service-support-hero.jpg`, `service-project-hero.jpg`, `service-consulting-hero.jpg`

### 6. Trust Indicators (Future)
**Location:** Homepage trust section (if added)
**Recommended:**
- Client logos (if available)
- Certification badges
- Industry awards
- **Files:** `client-logos/` directory

## Image Specifications

### Hero Images
- **Size:** 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)
- **Format:** JPG (optimized) or WebP
- **File Size:** < 200KB
- **Alt Text:** Descriptive, SEO-friendly

### Content Images
- **Size:** 1200x800px (3:2 ratio) or square
- **Format:** JPG (optimized) or WebP  
- **File Size:** < 150KB
- **Alt Text:** Descriptive

### Service Card Images
- **Size:** 800x600px (4:3 ratio)
- **Format:** JPG (optimized) or WebP
- **File Size:** < 100KB
- **Alt Text:** Service-specific

## Image Sources (Free/Stock)
- Unsplash (unsplash.com) - Free high-quality photos
- Pexels (pexels.com) - Free stock photos
- Pixabay (pixabay.com) - Free images
- Search terms: "IT support", "business technology", "team collaboration", "office workspace"

## Image Optimization Tools
- TinyPNG (tinypng.com) - Compress images
- Squoosh (squoosh.app) - Web-based optimization
- ImageOptim - Desktop app

## Accessibility
- Always include descriptive alt text
- Use appropriate file formats
- Ensure images don't slow page load
- Consider lazy loading for below-fold images
