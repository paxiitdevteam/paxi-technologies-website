## Scope of IT Project Management Services

This section describes the IT Project Management Services included in this proposal. Paxi Technologies will own and manage the project management function for this initiative.

### Project Planning and Scoping

Paxi Technologies will own project planning and scoping to define objectives, requirements, and boundaries. This ensures everyone understands what success looks like before work begins.

### Timeline and Milestone Control

Paxi Technologies will establish and control project timelines and milestones to ensure delivery stays on track and risks are identified early. Delays are identified and addressed proactively to keep the project on schedule.

### Vendor Coordination

When multiple vendors are involved, Paxi Technologies will coordinate all vendors and manage work between parties. This ensures all vendors are aligned with project goals and timelines.

### Stakeholder Communication

Paxi Technologies will provide regular status updates and ensure decision-makers have the information they need. This keeps stakeholders informed and engaged throughout the project.

### Delivery Oversight

Paxi Technologies will oversee project execution from start to finish, ensuring coordination and accountability throughout. We maintain visibility into project progress, confirm alignment with defined requirements, and maintain visibility into risks and dependencies.

## Project Governance

### Single Point of Accountability

Paxi Technologies serves as the single point of accountability for this project. [Client Name] has one person responsible for project success. There is no confusion about who owns what, no passing responsibility between vendors, and no gaps in ownership.

When questions arise or problems occur, [Client Name] knows who to contact and who will resolve them.

### Decision Authority and Escalation

Paxi Technologies will manage project decisions within defined authority levels:

- **Project Management Decisions**: Paxi Technologies will make decisions within the scope of project management activities
- **Client Decisions**: Decisions requiring client approval will be escalated promptly with recommendations
- **Vendor Decisions**: Vendor-related decisions will be coordinated through Paxi Technologies

Escalation paths are defined to ensure decisions are made promptly and appropriately.

## Delivery Approach

### Defined Scope

Every project begins with clearly defined scope, objectives, and deliverables. This foundation prevents confusion and ensures everyone understands what success looks like.

### Structured Checkpoints

Regular checkpoints provide visibility into project progress and identify issues early. [Client Name] always knows where the project stands and what's happening next.

### Change Control

Paxi Technologies manages changes through a structured process. Change requests are evaluated for impact on timeline and budget, approved before work begins, and documented clearly.

No surprises, no unauthorized scope expansion.

### Internal Delivery Discipline

To ensure consistent service delivery, Paxi Technologies uses an internal path management system to coordinate internal workflows, route service and project work correctly, and prevent operational bottlenecks. This system supports delivery efficiency but is not a customer-facing product or service.
